Wine Quest

150 gold-3 wild boars

Keenbear hasn't recieved any wine from the town vineyard for weeks now but is unable to go because hes the only employee. He tells the team he'll pay them to go see whats up. (50 gold) The team sets off south and takes the mountain road up to the vineyard. Upon arrival John greets them and takes them to the compound. Adriel and Vin invite them in for a tasting using their decanter of endless wine. Cant tend the fields because of wild boar infestation. Gored John. Party stands watch until Boars arrive. Fights Boar. Gets 100 gold from vineyard and magic flask. return to town tell Keenbear the good news.

Rescue Guards Quest

500 gold-2 wolves-3 bandits-1 mimic creature

There has been bandit activity and the woods are wild so anything could have happened. team sets off If they take the road nothing till cave. if they go through woods encounter wolves. fight win-leads to cave. enter cave. Two gaurds chit chatting , one cooking dinner. fight or talk, pay off for 300 gold. if payed off-bandits go back loudly and startle creature and it retreats into cave. if killed bandits. go back and fight mimic monster. rescue guards and one is infected!
